What is Mapo?

Snap messages on a shared map with the peple around you.


Got a new sofa and left the old one in te street..?
Take a picture of it and snap it's location on the map!
Now people in the neighborhood know about it!

Books, furniture, clothes, electric appliances... maybe someone around you would be happy knowing if it's out there.

All you need to do is:

  • open MAPO.
  • click on the "+" button.
  • snap a new temporary marker to the shared map.
It's as simple as that!


The more you use our service - the longer your markers stay on the map and your view area range will grow!


Local business can contact Mapo and get a special publicity service to target their local custumers.
